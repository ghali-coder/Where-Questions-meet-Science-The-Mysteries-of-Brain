The Mysteries of the Brain

Key Scientific Summaries

Sleep & Dreams

Why the brain never rests

Sleep is an active biological process. During deep sleep, the brain clears waste through the glymphatic system, restores energy, and strengthens memory. REM sleep supports dreaming, emotional processing, and learning.

Learning & Memory

The brain rewires itself

Learning changes the brain through neuroplasticity. Repetition strengthens neural connections, while sleep stabilizes them. Forgetting removes unused pathways to maintain efficiency.

Social Influence

How others affect us

Social evaluation activates the amygdala and stress hormones, which can reduce focus. Positive feedback releases dopamine, improving motivation and confidence.

Music & Emotion

Why music moves us

Music activates the brain’s reward system, regulates stress, synchronizes movement, and stimulates emotional and imaginative brain areas.
